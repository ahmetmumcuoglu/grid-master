HOW TO PLAY

Build words horizontally (left-to-right) and vertically (top-to-bottom) on the 5x5 grid.

  • The game generates 24 random letters each day: 14 consonants and 10 vowels.
  • Tap a cell to place the random letter and tap again to confirm the placement.
  • On your final (25th) turn, you will choose a Joker letter from the keyboard to complete the board.
  • Rule: Each letter on the grid can only be used in one word per row and one word per column.

Scoring

2 Letters2 pts
3 Letters5 pts
4 Letters9 pts
5 Letters15 pts

The game automatically calculates the highest-scoring combination of words in each row and column.
